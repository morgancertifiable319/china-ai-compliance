## 📋 About china-ai-compliance

This application helps people working with artificial intelligence in China meet government rules. It covers important tasks like filing AI algorithms, registering large models, labeling AI-generated content, handling data correctly, and managing international compliance. The tool is made by a lawyer with a computer science background. It comes with regular updates and supports Chinese and English.

The app focuses on making compliance clear without complicated steps or legal jargon. It suits AI developers, product managers, legal teams, or anyone who wants to make sure AI projects follow China’s fast-changing laws and policies.
